About Me

My name is Odysseas Katsougrakis. I am a front-end web developer based in Brooklyn, New York. I graduated from CUNY Hunter College in 2017 with a B.A. in Computer Science, and since then I have spent my time self-teaching in order to learn above and beyond what I already know. I am extremely passionate about programming and am very eager to get hands-on experience working with front-end technologies.

I am currently working as a Production Support Analyst, and during my free time I enjoy coming up with new ideas for web applications to create. Choosing amongst the many different ways to implement an application is probably one of the most thrilling parts of being a developer, and once I have an idea in mind I do my best to bring it to life. For this reason, I'd like to make my way into a tech company and bring my ideas along with me.

My skills include:

C++ | HTML | CSS | JavaScript | SQL

Python | Bootstrap | jQuery | OOP
